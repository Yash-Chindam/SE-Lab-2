Anime refers to a style of animation that originated in Japan and has become popular worldwide. It encompasses a wide range of genres and themes, including action, adventure, romance, fantasy, science fiction, horror, and more. Anime can be produced in various formats, including television series, films, and web series. It often features colorful artwork, fantastical themes, exaggerated expressions, and vibrant characters. Anime has a diverse audience, spanning different age groups and cultural backgrounds, and has gained significant recognition for its storytelling, creativity, and visual aesthetics.
